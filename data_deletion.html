Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how you can request the deletion of any personal data we have collected through our application.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you wish to request that we delete any personal data associated with your use of our application, please send an email to:

kata@hey.com

In your email, please include your unique identifier or any relevant details that can help us locate your data. We will process your request in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

What Happens Next?

Once we receive your request, we will verify your identity as required and then proceed to delete your personal data from our records. We will notify you via email once the deletion is complete.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please feel free to contact us at the email address above.

Thank you for helping us maintain a transparent and secure data handling process.
